In-the-wild Dataset

A hand-annotated set of splices collected from "in-the-wild" photoshops. Images were selected from Reddit and the Onion and annotated by the authors. Because the images come from the internet, the ground truth masks may not capture all manipulations, only the significant ones. As such, they are suitable for benchmarking but not necessarily training.
